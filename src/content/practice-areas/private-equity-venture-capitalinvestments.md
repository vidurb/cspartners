The Firm advises various venture capital firms, hedge funds, private equity funds, mezzanine funds and infrastructure funds on structuring investment vehicles. Our team works with a variety of funds, companies, and investors. Our practical and pragmatic approach has enabled our clients to steer through various complicated transactions and the team has assisted its clients through the full life cycle of investments.

Our private equity and venture capital practice is designed to support investors and portfolio companies through every stage of the investment cycle—from fund structuring and deal sourcing to exits and post‑investment governance. We also advise start-ups seeking assistance and advisory on VC investments.

We assist clients in evaluating investment opportunities, conducting legal due diligence, and negotiating transaction documents that balance investor protections with commercial flexibility required by the startup.

Our team understands the unique dynamics of growth‑stage investments, including valuation considerations, founder rights, liquidation preferences, and governance structures.

We ensure that investment frameworks are robust, scalable, and aligned with long‑term value creation. Beyond deal execution, we support clients in managing ongoing compliance, board governance, shareholder relations, and restructuring events. Our experience across sectors such as technology, healthcare, consumer goods, and infrastructure allows us to provide practical insights into market trends and regulatory developments. We also advise on exit strategies, including secondary sales, strategic acquisitions, and IPO‑linked transitions. With a focus on clarity, responsiveness, and strategic foresight, our practice helps investors and companies navigate the complexities of the investment ecosystem with confidence.
