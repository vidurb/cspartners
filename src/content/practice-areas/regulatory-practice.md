Our regulatory practice supports clients navigating India’s complex and evolving regulatory framework, combining technical regulatory expertise with practical, sector-specific insight. We frequently advise on regulatory frameworks, prescribed by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), Reserve Bank of India (RBI), Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) and other sector specific regulatory bodies. We advise on legal issues, as well as ongoing investigations and enforcement proceedings. Our assignments often involve international or cross-border elements, and we have significant experience advising on regulatory issues involving multiple jurisdictions.

Our regulatory practice is designed to help clients operate confidently within India’s dynamic and increasingly stringent compliance environment. As regulatory frameworks evolve to address emerging business models, technological innovation, and global standards, companies often face uncertainty in interpreting and implementing new requirements.

We assist clients in understanding these changes, assessing their impact, and adopting compliance strategies that are both practical and aligned with business objectives. Our team regularly advises on governance structures, disclosure obligations, reporting requirements, and sector-specific compliance issues, ensuring that clients remain ahead of regulatory expectations.

We also support clients during regulatory inspections, audits, and enforcement actions, providing end-to-end assistance from initial response to final resolution. Our experience across multiple regulators enables us to anticipate areas of concern and prepare clients for interactions with authorities. In cross-border matters, we help clients navigate the interplay between Indian regulations and foreign legal frameworks, particularly in areas such as securities, financial services, and corporate governance. With a focus on clarity, responsiveness, and strategic foresight, our regulatory practice ensures that clients can manage compliance risks effectively while continuing to pursue growth and innovation.
