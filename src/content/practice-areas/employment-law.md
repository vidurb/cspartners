The Firm helps organisations navigate the new labour codes with practical, business focused guidance. Our team advises on wage structuring, social security coverage, working hours, termination processes, and compliance timelines so companies can implement changes smoothly and reduce disruption.

We conduct end-to-end employment compliance review across HR documentation, contracts, payroll structures, and statutory benefit obligations. These reviews identify gaps such as incorrect classification of employees, benefit mismatches, vendor risks, and termination exposures, allowing businesses to address issues before they escalate.

We support employers in restructuring payroll frameworks to align with legal requirements while managing financial impact. This includes provident fund and gratuity coverage, overtime and leave encashment practices, and variable pay policies, along with phased transition plans that ensure continuity.

Our ongoing advisory covers policy drafting, periodic compliance reviews, investigations into workplace misconduct, grievance redressal mechanisms, whistleblower frameworks, and representation before authorities. The objective is to help organisations build compliant, fair, and accountable workplaces with strong governance and legal safeguards.

Our employment law practice is designed to help organisations build resilient HR frameworks that balance legal compliance with operational efficiency. We work closely with HR teams, leadership, and compliance officers to ensure that employment policies reflect current legal requirements and industry best practices. Our team assists in drafting employment contracts, handbooks, and workplace policies that are clear, enforceable, and tailored to organisational needs. We also advise on sensitive matters such as disciplinary actions, workplace investigations, and employee exits, ensuring that processes are fair, defensible, and aligned with statutory obligations.

In addition to advisory support, we help organisations prepare for regulatory inspections, respond to notices, and manage disputes before labour authorities and courts. Our experience across industries enables us to understand sector‑specific workforce challenges, from gig‑economy models to hybrid work structures. We also assist clients in implementing training programs, compliance audits, and governance frameworks that promote transparency and accountability. With a focus on practicality, clarity, and risk mitigation, our employment practice helps organisations foster compliant, productive, and inclusive workplaces.
