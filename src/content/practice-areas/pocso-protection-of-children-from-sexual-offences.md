We provide clear advisory support on compliance with the POCSO Act, including reporting obligations, response protocols, coordination with authorities, and documentation standards. The focus is on preventing risk, avoiding procedural lapses, and ensuring sensitive, lawful handling of incidents.

We conduct awareness and training sessions that explain how to recognise warning signs, respond to disclosures, and create safe environments for children. Such awareness and training sessions enhance understanding of statutory duties, confidentiality protocols, and respectful communication with affected individuals.

We draft child protection and safeguarding policies that set out reporting channels, responsibility structures, conduct guidelines, and incident management frameworks. Each policy is designed to be clear, workable, and compliant while strengthening day to day safety practices.

Our POCSO advisory practice is built on the understanding that organisations working with children carry heightened responsibilities and must maintain rigorous safeguards. We assist schools, NGOs, residential facilities, sports academies, and other child‑facing institutions in developing comprehensive protection frameworks that minimise risks and ensure compliance with statutory obligations. Our team helps organisations establish clear reporting hierarchies, emergency response protocols, and documentation standards that withstand regulatory scrutiny. We also advise on coordination with law enforcement, child welfare committees, and other authorities to ensure that responses are timely, lawful, and sensitive.

In addition to advisory support, we conduct capacity‑building programs for teachers, staff, volunteers, and administrators. These sessions focus on recognising behavioural indicators, responding to disclosures with empathy, and maintaining confidentiality while fulfilling mandatory reporting duties. We also assist organisations in conducting internal audits, reviewing existing policies, and strengthening governance structures to prevent lapses. With a focus on clarity, sensitivity, and legal compliance, our POCSO practice helps institutions create safe, accountable, and child‑centric environments.
