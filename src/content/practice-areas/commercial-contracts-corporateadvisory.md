Our practice includes drafting, negotiating, and advising on a wide range of commercial arrangements such as shareholder agreements, business transfer agreements, distribution and supply contracts, technology and services agreements, and sector specific commercial documentation. Our Counsels have advised on commercial contracts across industries such as waste management, power plants, renewable energy to name a few. We focus on contracts that are clear, enforceable, and aligned with the client’s commercial strategy.

Our commercial advisory practice is built on the understanding that well‑structured contracts form the foundation of stable and successful business relationships.

We work closely with clients to understand their operational needs, risk appetite, and long‑term objectives, ensuring that each agreement reflects a balanced allocation of rights and obligations. Our team brings clarity to complex commercial arrangements by drafting documents that are precise, practical, and tailored to the realities of the client’s business. Whether negotiating with counterparties, addressing regulatory considerations, or aligning contractual terms with internal policies, we ensure that every contract supports the client’s strategic goals.

In addition to transactional support, we advise clients on day‑to‑day corporate governance, compliance frameworks, and operational decision‑making. This includes reviewing existing contracts for risk exposure, advising on contractual disputes, and helping businesses adapt to evolving legal and regulatory requirements. Our experience spans diverse industries, enabling us to provide sector‑specific insights and anticipate potential challenges. By combining legal expertise with commercial understanding, we help clients build resilient contractual frameworks that support growth, reduce disputes, and enhance operational efficiency.
