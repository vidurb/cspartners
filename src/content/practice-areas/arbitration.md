C&S Partners provides expert legal services in Domestic and International Commercial Arbitration for both domestic and foreign clients. Our team possesses experience in sector-specific arbitrations, including Construction, Energy, Sale of Goods and Services, Shareholder Disputes, and Insurance.

The firm represents clients in both ad hoc and institutional arbitration forums across domestic and international jurisdictions. We manage all phases of the arbitration process, from initiation to representation before forums challenging awards. Additionally, we develop comprehensive strategies for effective enforcement and timely resolution of proceedings.

Our team prides on its in-depth knowledge, both academic and practical. The team keeps itself abreast of the changing facets of the legal landscape relating to Arbitration and Contract Law so that the legal advice and strategy provided to the clients is not outdated. The core philosophy of the arbitration practice being to devise a strategy which resolves the disputes efficiently and helping clients achieve favourable outcomes.

Our arbitration practice is built on a deep understanding of the commercial realities that shape disputes. We recognise that arbitration is not merely a procedural alternative to litigation but a strategic forum where industry knowledge, clarity of presentation, and tactical foresight can significantly influence outcomes. Our lawyers work closely with clients to understand the commercial context of each dispute, enabling us to craft submissions and arguments that resonate with arbitral tribunals. Whether the matter involves complex contractual interpretation, technical evidence, or multi-party claims, we ensure that every stage of the process is handled with precision and strategic intent.

In addition to representing clients in hearings, we assist in drafting arbitration agreements, advising on seat and venue considerations, and evaluating the enforceability of awards across jurisdictions. Our team is adept at navigating the interplay between arbitration and court proceedings, particularly in matters involving interim relief, appointment of arbitrators, and challenges to awards. With a commitment to efficiency and clarity, we aim to reduce procedural delays and ensure that clients benefit from the speed and flexibility that arbitration promises. Our approach is grounded in responsiveness, meticulous preparation, and a strong understanding of global arbitration trends, allowing us to deliver solutions that are both legally sound and commercially aligned.
