Our lawyers advise clients on the full spectrum of matters under the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) regulatory framework, including framework for listed companies and capital market participants. We have significant experience representing clients on matters involving SEBI Take-Over Code, SEBI’s framework on Insider Trading, Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements, and matters involving allegations of market manipulation. We also advise listed companies and directors on corporate governance, board compositions, compliance, issues regarding related party transactions, disclosures, and on SEBI investigations and actions.

Our capital markets regulatory practice is grounded in a deep understanding of the complexities of securities law and the practical realities of operating in India’s fast-evolving market ecosystem. We assist clients in interpreting regulatory developments, structuring transactions, and ensuring compliance with disclosure and governance norms. Our team works closely with listed entities, intermediaries, and market participants to address issues relating to insider trading controls, market conduct, and takeover obligations.

We also help clients design internal compliance frameworks that minimise regulatory exposure and support transparent, well-governed operations. In contentious matters, we represent clients in responding to show-cause notices, preparing submissions, and engaging with SEBI during investigations and enforcement proceedings. Our experience allows us to anticipate regulatory concerns and craft strategies that balance legal defence with reputational considerations. We also advise independent directors and senior management on their personal liabilities, fiduciary duties, and obligations under securities law. With a focus on clarity, precision, and strategic insight, our practice ensures that clients receive comprehensive support across both advisory and enforcement-related aspects of securities regulation.
