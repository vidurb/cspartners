Our team has been at the forefront of high-profile insolvency resolution processes and has been a part of resolution of some of the ‘Dirty Dozen’ assets including the resolution of Bhushan Steel, Amtek Auto & Essar Steel, among others. The team also advised one of the stakeholders in the first telecom sector insolvency. Most recently, the team has also been involved in resolution processes for infrastructure companies, advising various stakeholders such as the suspended directors, homeowners associations, secured creditors and individual homeowners.

Members of the team have hands-on experience of acting for resolution applicants and provide pragmatic and solution-oriented approach to the high-risk items identified during the due diligence process. Our Insolvency practice offers comprehensive assistance to creditors, debtors, and other stakeholders in navigating the insolvency and bankruptcy landscape. We provide effective strategies for debt resolution, corporate restructuring, and insolvency proceedings, protecting the interests of our clients.

Our insolvency and bankruptcy practice is grounded in a deep understanding of the commercial, financial, and regulatory complexities that shape distressed situations. We assist clients in evaluating distressed assets, assessing viability, and formulating resolution strategies that balance legal requirements with commercial realities. Our team works closely with resolution applicants, creditors, resolution professionals and other stakeholders to navigate the procedural intricacies of the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC), including committee of creditors processes, information memorandum reviews, and negotiation of resolution plans. We bring clarity and structure to high‑pressure situations, ensuring that clients make informed decisions at every stage.

In addition to resolution processes, we advise on pre‑insolvency restructuring, enforcement of security interests, and litigation before NCLT, NCLAT, and other forums. Our experience across sectors such as steel, automotive, telecom, and infrastructure, enables us to understand industry‑specific challenges and tailor our strategies accordingly. We also assist clients in managing regulatory interactions, addressing operational continuity issues, and mitigating litigation risks. With a focus on strategic thinking, meticulous preparation, and practical problem‑solving, our insolvency practice helps clients navigate financial distress with clarity and confidence.
