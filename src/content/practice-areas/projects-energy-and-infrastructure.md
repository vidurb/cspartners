In the Projects practice at the firm, we provide strategic counsel to clients in respect of regulatory aspects of various projects across sectors such as Power, Renewable Energy, Airports, Highways, Metro Rail Projects among others. Counsels at the firm have advised contractors, sponsors, and lenders on project development and financing in infrastructure and energy sectors. Further, the Counsels have advised prospective bidders in relation to various bids for solar power projects invited as part of the National Solar Mission and certain state tenders.

We have also acted as counsel for various concessioning authorities and have robust experience in preparation of bid documents, project agreements as well as advising on project management issues. We also assist our clients with risk assessment and evaluation of various project documents.

Our lawyers have a deep understanding of the sector's challenges and opportunities, guiding clients through regulatory hurdles and contractual complexities. Our projects and infrastructure practice is built on a comprehensive understanding of the regulatory, financial, and operational frameworks that govern large‑scale infrastructure development.

We assist clients in structuring projects, negotiating concession agreements, and navigating approval processes across multiple authorities. Our team works closely with sponsors, lenders, EPC contractors, and government bodies to ensure that project documentation is robust, bankable, and aligned with sector‑specific requirements. Whether advising on renewable energy projects, transportation networks, or urban infrastructure, we bring clarity and strategic insight to complex, multi‑stakeholder engagements.

In addition to transactional support, we advise on risk allocation, performance obligations, and dispute avoidance mechanisms throughout the project lifecycle. Our experience in bid advisory, project finance, and regulatory compliance enables us to anticipate challenges and propose practical solutions that support timely execution. We also assist clients in addressing environmental, land acquisition, and operational issues that often arise in large‑scale projects. With a focus on precision, sectoral expertise, and long‑term planning, our practice helps clients deliver sustainable and commercially viable infrastructure outcomes.
