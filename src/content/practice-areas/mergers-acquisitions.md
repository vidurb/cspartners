Our team advises on complex domestic and cross border M&A transactions, including some of the largest deals in key sectors. We assist clients through every stage – structuring, due diligence, negotiation, documentation, regulatory approvals, and post-closing integration. The firm’s experience spans retail, e-commerce, pharmaceuticals, financial services, technology, and manufacturing. The Firm has a robust M&A Practice, having advised on several complex and market leading transactions, in a wide range of sectors. The transactions advised on include, outright acquisitions, mergers, joint ventures, minority stake acquisitions, exit by shareholders and PE/VC investments. The Firm advises on an end-to end basis, including due diligences, structuring, transactions documentation, compliance and on corporate governance issues. The Firm has advised on sectors such as Automobiles, Pharmaceuticals, Information Technology, Diagnostics, Infrastructure and Hospitality.

Our M&A practice is grounded in a deep understanding of commercial objectives, regulatory frameworks, and the strategic motivations that drive transactions. We work closely with clients to identify risks, evaluate synergies, and structure deals that are both legally sound and commercially advantageous.

Our team is adept at navigating complex negotiations, aligning diverse stakeholder interests, and ensuring that transaction documents reflect the commercial intent with clarity and precision. Whether advising on strategic acquisitions, cross‑border investments, or multi‑party joint ventures, we bring a solution‑oriented approach that anticipates challenges and supports seamless execution.

Beyond transaction closure, we assist clients with integration planning, governance alignment, and post‑closing compliance to ensure long‑term success. Our experience across industries enables us to understand sector‑specific regulatory nuances, competitive landscapes, and operational considerations. We also support clients in managing regulatory interactions, including filings with the Competition Commission of India, RBI, SEBI, and other authorities. With a focus on responsiveness, strategic insight, and meticulous execution, our M&A practice helps clients pursue growth, consolidation, and transformation with confidence.
