We regularly advise on Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA) and the regulations framed under it, including those governing foreign direct investment (FDI) and overseas investment structures, regulatory approvals and ongoing compliance. We assist clients through all stages of cross-border transactions, from transaction structuring, regulatory advice, documentation and post-investment compliance, including ensuring adherence to sectoral caps and optimising investment structures within India’s foreign exchange regulatory framework.

Our FEMA and investment advisory practice is designed to support clients navigating the complexities of India’s foreign exchange regime, which continues to evolve in response to global economic trends and domestic policy priorities.

We assist clients in structuring inbound and outbound investments in a manner that is both compliant and commercially efficient. This includes advising on sectoral restrictions, pricing guidelines, reporting obligations, and the regulatory implications of various investment instruments. Our team works closely with clients to anticipate regulatory hurdles and design structures that minimise compliance risks while supporting long-term business objectives.

We also provide end-to-end support during cross-border transactions, including drafting transaction documents, liaising with authorised dealers, and preparing filings with regulatory authorities. Our experience spans a wide range of industries, enabling us to offer sector-specific insights and practical solutions. In addition to transactional support, we assist clients in responding to regulatory queries, managing compounding proceedings, and addressing issues arising from inadvertent non-compliance. With a focus on clarity, responsiveness, and strategic foresight, our practice ensures that clients can navigate India’s foreign exchange landscape with confidence and efficiency.
