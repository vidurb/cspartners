The Firm has varied experience in handling disputes relating to white collar crimes. The Senior Partners, Partners and the other team members have extensively advised the clients for smooth handling of the investigation process. The Firm’s aim has always been to create a balance between complete co-operation with the investigating agencies and parallelly also protecting the interest of the client.

C&S Partners has a robust White Collar Crime practice with deep experience representing clients in complex, high stakes investigations and enforcement actions across India. The firm has successfully defended leading fintech and financial services companies in proceedings before the Enforcement Directorate, including matters involving illegal attachment of nodal accounts and excess monies without due process.

The team regularly appears before ED, CBI, EOWs, and various courts, securing relief in defreezing proceedings, quashing FIRs, and navigating parallel regulatory and criminal exposure. Its work spans sensitive mandates for Indian subsidiaries of global corporations, major FMCG groups, and multinational technology and telecom companies facing allegations of fraud, money laundering, and other economic offences.

Beyond defence strategy, C&S Partners advises on corporate investigations, crisis management, and internal compliance risks. The firm has led whistle blower investigations for prominent consumer goods companies, guided real estate developers and consultancy firms through proceedings before the PMLA Appellate Tribunal, and acted in fraud matters involving sums exceeding INR 100 crores. The practice is known for its strategic, discreet, and outcome-oriented approach, particularly in matters where legal, commercial, and reputational stakes intersect.

Our white-collar defence practice is built on the understanding that investigations today are multi-dimensional, often involving simultaneous scrutiny from enforcement agencies, regulators, auditors, and internal stakeholders. We assist clients in navigating this complex landscape by offering clear, practical, and timely advice at every stage, from responding to summons and notices to managing search and seizure actions, freezing orders, and cross-border information requests. Our team is adept at identifying procedural irregularities, safeguarding constitutional rights, and ensuring that investigative processes remain fair and within the bounds of law. We also work closely with forensic experts, auditors, and compliance teams to build a robust factual foundation for defence.

In addition to handling active investigations, we help clients strengthen their internal systems to mitigate future risks. This includes advising on compliance frameworks, conducting internal audits, and training senior management on regulatory expectations. Our experience across industries allows us to understand sector-specific vulnerabilities and tailor our advice accordingly. We recognise that white-collar matters often carry significant reputational consequences, and therefore adopt a discreet, strategic, and solution-oriented approach. Whether the objective is to secure early closure, negotiate settlements, or mount a strong defence in court, we ensure that clients receive comprehensive support that balances legal, commercial, and reputational considerations.
