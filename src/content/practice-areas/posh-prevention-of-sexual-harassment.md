We design POSH frameworks that create safe, respectful, and legally compliant workplaces. This includes drafting comprehensive POSH policies, defining reporting and inquiry processes, and aligning governance with statutory requirements.

We assist organisations in setting up Internal Committees and training members on inquiry handling, evidence assessment, confidentiality, and due process. Where needed, we conduct and / or aid independent investigations and prepare clear, defensible findings and recommendations.

We conduct workplace awareness programs covering respectful conduct, complaint handling, gender sensitivity, digital behaviour, and employee rights. Our programs are customised for various workforce sizes, industry, and remote or hybrid models. We also support statutory and procedural compliance, including annual POSH filings, record management, and audit readiness. Our counsels serve as external committee members to ensure fairness, neutrality, and adherence to legal standards while guiding organisations through sensitive matters.

Our POSH practice is grounded in the belief that safe workplaces are essential for organisational integrity, employee well‑being, and long‑term productivity. We help organisations build systems that not only comply with the law but also foster trust, transparency, and accountability. Our team works closely with leadership and HR departments to design reporting mechanisms that are accessible, confidential, and sensitive to the needs of complainants. We also assist in creating communication frameworks that encourage employees to speak up without fear of retaliation, ensuring that workplace culture supports dignity and respect.

Beyond policy design, we provide hands‑on support during inquiries, ensuring that Internal Committees follow due process, maintain neutrality, and document proceedings meticulously. Our training programs equip committee members with the skills needed to handle complex cases, assess evidence, and draft reasoned findings. We also advise organisations on preventive strategies, including behavioural guidelines, digital conduct norms, and leadership sensitisation. With a focus on fairness, clarity, and legal compliance, our POSH practice helps organisations create workplaces where employees feel protected, valued, and empowered.
